Education

  • Kindergarten

    During my kindergarten years, I don’t remember where I studied at. Mainly because the kindergarten already moved to another places, and the place are sold to another family.

    2005-2006

  • Primary school

    I studied at Methodist Girls Primary School Ipoh. During my years there, there were two session, a morning (for fourth year until sixth year students) and evening class (for first- and third-year student). My class stayed the same for five year and during my last year, I moved one class back. I remember as a student, I wasn’t a diligent student but I finished the school without any problem. After UPSR, I remember joining a “senamrobik” competition, unfortunately, my class won the third place.

    2007-2012

  • Secondary School

    I continue my study at Methodist Girl’s Secondary School, this school is just next to the primary school. One unique feature about my school is that there is no building separating the primary to the secondary school. We even share the canteen and main hall. Our class are in the same building but different floor. In my form 3, I joined the environmental club and joined them going to Tempurung Cave. Form 3 also I joined a three-day camp, and it was my best memories, to bad I didn’t bring my phones and there weren’t many photos of us there

    2013-2017

  • University Year

    When I first come to UiTM Kedah, it was a totally new environment. I met so many amazing new people. I also create so many memories. Being in university, I joined Balloon Charity Run. I also join so many events. Not only that, I got the opportunity to be a facilitator for an event with outside school students. I was supposed to do another event during my fourth semester, however, pandemic hits us and we couldn’t continue doing it.

    2018-2021
